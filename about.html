FauDroids

FauDroids is a personal challenge where we develop one Android app every month in 2015 (think of it as a new years resolution). MrHyde was our app for April.

Find out more about FauDroids at http://faudroids.org our have a look at our other apps on the Google Play Store.

Also, everything about MrHyde is open source, so check it out on GitHub!
