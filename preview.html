Previews

Writing your posts in Markdown is one thing, but actually seeing your blog entry before publishing it is second to none to get a first impression of it.

In order to give you the possibility to preview your posts we built a web service which allows you to preview your post.

Whenever you want to preview changes in a post, a diff file of your local changes is created and sent to our webservice. Our server then creates a temporary clone of your GitHub repository and patches it using the diff file.
After the changes have been applied, we fire up Jekyll to generate your site, which is then published using dynamic subdomains.
Since our storage is limited we're deleting every preview after 10 minutes.
